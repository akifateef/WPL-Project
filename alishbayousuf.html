Mini Bio

Alishba Yousuf is a Pakistani VJ, model and actress who works in Pakistani serials and telefilms. She is best known for her roles in serial Main Abdul Qadir Hoon (2010), Takkay ki Ayegi Baraat (2011), Meray Dard Ko Jo Zuban Miley (2012), Annie ki Ayegi Baraat (2012), and Tanhaiyan Naye Silsilay (2012).Alishba was born on September 16, 1985 and was brought up in a Muslim family in Karachi, Pakistan. She is the sister of VJ Palwasha Yousuf and actress Syra Yousuf.On 12 February 2009, she married Rayyan Durrani (who appeared in MasterChef Pakistan) in an Islamic wedding ceremony in Karachi.The couple have a daughter named Iliyana.
