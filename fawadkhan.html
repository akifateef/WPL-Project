Fawad Khan


Biography


Overview

Birth Name Fawad Afzal Khan
Height 5' 11" (1.8 m)


Mini Bio

Fawad Afzal Khan is an actor, model and a singer. He is most widely recognized for his performance in the 2007 movie Khuda Kay Liye by Shoaib Mansoor and his widely popular drama aired in 2010, Dastaan and Humsafar in 2011-12. He is also widely known for being the lead singer of Pakistani rock act EP.
